Women’s Restorative Centre

Women’s Restorative Centre is a psychological service that is dedicated to maintain and to restore women’s mental health. Women’s Restorative Centre is unique because we are sensitive to the unique challenges that women, which equips us to validate and address women’s concerns. We treat each woman as an individual and we tailor counselling to each woman’s concern. Women’s Restorative Centre will work diligently with you to help you address short or long-term life challenges, such as:
• Depression Anxiety Trauma\PTSD Stress Obsessive compulsive disorder Career and work Communication problems Coping skills Relationship problems Struggles with addictions Finding purpose and meaning Adjustment issues Anger management Retirement anxiety Self esteem Self-harm
