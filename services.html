Appointments

Sessions for individuals are 60 minutes long; assessments may take longer, depending on your concerns.

In certain circumstances, we may decide we wish to have longer sessions together, and this can be easily accommodated.

To arrange an appointment please email me At heroncounselling@yahoo.com or call my office at 416-843-9309.

Cost

For individuals, the cost is $150/60 minute session (includes HST).

If you must cancel or reschedule your appointment, please contact me within 24 hours prior to the session to avoid being charged your full session rate.

Payment

Fees are payable by cheque or cash at the beginning of the session. Alternatively, payment may be made by electronic transfer prior to our session.

Psychotherapy services provided by private practitioners are not covered by OHIP but may be covered by private insurance plans. Please check with your insurance policy administrator.

Cancellation Policy

All sessions must be cancelled 24 hours in advance. Full fees will be charged for cancellations that are less than 24 hours.
